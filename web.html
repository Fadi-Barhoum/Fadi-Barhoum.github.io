SSDF

site that enables beneficiaries to submit projects to the SSDF Charitable Committee for evaluation and decision regarding financing.
Management can be done through the control panel:
- Add beneficiaries to submit their projects.
- Add and control questions as in Google Forms.
- Requesting additional matters from the beneficiaries, questions, and documents.
- Project evaluation.
- Add outgoing payments to beneficiaries.
- View the funding volume for each project and all projects and the remaining amounts.
- Conduct follow-ups through follow-up forms added by the administration.
- Add publications to the news page.
- Adding and modifying board members.
- Add and edit volunteers.
- The site also arranges projects automatically according to the questions set by the administration.
Note: The site is finished and we are waiting for the content from the SSDF management to add it to the site and upload to the server.
